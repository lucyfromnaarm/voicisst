# Code of Conduct

Flow is built by and for people who need their computers to meet them where
they are — including people with chronic illness, limited energy, and limited
mobility. The community around it should work the same way.

## Expectations

- Be kind. Be patient. Assume good faith.
- Accessibility feedback is first-class feedback. "This is hard for me to
  use" is always a valid issue.
- Respect people's limits. Contributors here may have fluctuating capacity;
  "no response yet" never deserves a nudge-storm.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks, in any project space.
- Disagree with ideas, not people; when in doubt, ask a question instead of
  making an accusation.

## Scope and enforcement

This applies to all project spaces: issues, pull requests, discussions, and
any chat connected to the project. Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove
content and restrict participation when these expectations are broken,
escalating from a warning to a temporary or permanent ban as needed.

Report problems privately to luke@octavia.one. Reports are handled
confidentially, and reporters will never face retaliation for a good-faith
report.
